Geometrical Optics

thin lens

  • two axises pointing to opposite directions.
    image at object space, \(d_i<0\).
  • concave: \(f<0\), convex:\(f>0\)
  • The refraction is happened at the center plane
thin lens equation: $$\frac{1}{f}=\frac{1}{d_i}+\frac{1}{d_o}$$ $$M=\frac{h_i}{h_o} = -\frac{d_i}{d_o}$$

concave mirror

$$\frac{1}{d_i}+\frac{1}{d_o}=\frac{2}{R}$$

  • Always has a real image
